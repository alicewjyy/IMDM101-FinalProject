IMDM101 Final Project
Name: Galactic Compass
Collaborators: William Zheng, Avelyne Tran, Alice Wang, Luis Cortez

The Project Idea:
  In Galactic Compass, you start out in a spaceship that has been left stranded in space with no way to get back to Earth (so you think).
  You (the player) are left with one option; to explore the nearby planets with a small emergency pod your ship has. The original idea
  is to have a small, simple crafting system that they player can make new tools with, while also collecting fauna to decorate the main
  ship (make it look pretty while you try to find a way back home). The end goal is to collect enough resources to make an alternate fuel
  source that you can use to power your ship back home.

Current Status:
  Due to the time constraints, we are only going to implement one planet into Galactic Compass. Had we more time, we would have liked to
  implemented two or three planets. That way the player could collect varying resources in each planet and experiment with the materials
  collected to try and create that alternate fuel source.

  A good chunk of the environmental modeling is done, with a focus on applying textures right now. We have most of the UIs completed as
  well; the in-ship HUD display, settings UI, and dialogue UI. The inventory system is still being prototyped and worked on, with
  implementation expected this weekend (~ May 2nd). The game is expected to be put all together early next week (~ May 5th) in preparation
  for the final presentation.
